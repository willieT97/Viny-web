Our Mission

At Viny, we believe that music thrives in communities, not just algorithms. Our mission is to reconnect people with their local music scenes — to make it easy to discover gigs, jam sessions, and hidden gems in your own town.

Whether you’re a listener, a musician, or somewhere in between, Viny helps you see what’s happening around you — and become part of it.

Know a session we should include? Want to bring Viny to your town? Reach out. We’d love to hear from you.
